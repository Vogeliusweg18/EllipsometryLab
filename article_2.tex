\begin{abstract}
\noindent \bfseries In this report we present the comparative study of adsorption of Bovin serum albumin (BSA) on two different substrates using Ellipsometry. Two substrates, Gold and TiO$_{2}$ have been used to study how the adhesion of protein depends on the surface of the material. After the protein is left to adsorb on the surface of the substrates, the thickness of protein layer(which directly reflects the degree of adhesion) on both the samples is measured using a spectrscopic ellipsometer. The results of the study have been presented in the report.   % Dummy abstract text - replace \blindtext with your abstract text
\end{abstract}
